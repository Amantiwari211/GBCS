This is About of Gyanodaya Bal Committee

Gyanodaya Bal Committee is the name of a group which is in Sarkar village. This group organizes festivals like Saraswati Puja, Diwali, Chhath, Durga Puja in the village. This group was founded by the ancestors of the village.
